Civic Complaint Management System

Our platform empowers citizens to report public issues directly and efficiently. Once a complaint is submitted, it is instantly received by the admin, who assigns the task to the appropriate field worker. The worker completes the job and updates the status in real-time, allowing citizens to track progress seamlessly.

Mission

Led by Tahsin Zaman, our mission is to ensure transparency, accountability, and quick resolution of civic complaints through real-time tracking and AI analysis.

Our Team

  • Admin: Receives complaints and assigns tasks
  • Worker: Resolves complaints and updates status
  • Citizen: Submits complaints and tracks progress

About Civic System

Smart Civic Complain Management System lets citizens report public issues quickly. Complaints are received by admins, assigned to workers, and tracked in real-time until resolved, ensuring fast and transparent service.
