Welcome To Civic Complain Management

Our platform empowers citizens to report public issues directly and efficiently, whether it is related to roads, streetlights, waste management, or other civic concerns. Citizens can submit complaints through a simple interface, attaching photos or descriptions, ensuring the problem is documented clearly. Once submitted, the complaint instantly reaches the admin, who reviews the information and prioritizes it based on urgency and type of issue. The admin then assigns the task to the most suitable field worker or team, ensuring the right personnel handle the problem promptly.

The assigned worker receives the task in real-time, goes to the location, resolves the issue, and updates the status in the system immediately. Citizens can track the progress of their complaint at every step, seeing updates such as "Task Assigned," "Worker En Route," and "Issue Resolved." This transparent process creates accountability at every level: the citizen knows their concern is being addressed, the admin can monitor performance, and the worker has clear instructions and timelines. By streamlining the collaboration between citizens, admins, and field workers, our system ensures public services are handled efficiently, responsibly, and in a way that builds trust in digital urban governance.
